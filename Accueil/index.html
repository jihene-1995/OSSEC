What is OSSEC ?



OSSEC (Open Source Software ENSI Club) is a club recently inaugurated at the National School of Computer Science (ENSI), our goal is to promote open source culture between ENSI students who basically Sharing and exchange. We aim to ensure a working and development environment that prepares our members for professional life.


What is ENSI ?


The National School of Computer Science or ENSI is a Tunisian school training engineers in information and communication technologies.


Our Formations

OSSEC provides numerous training courses within the ENSI including web workshops, android workshops, training in shell programming ... these courses aim to forge the student's skills and put into practice his theoretical knowledge.
